Learn to Dive – From First Bubbles to Dive Pro

Have you always dreamed of breathing underwater? At Chang Diving Center, we offer beginner-friendly scuba diving courses designed for safety, fun, and real progress – no experience needed!

Try your first breaths below the surface with our Try Dive experience – a relaxed 1-day introduction guided by our professional instructors. Want a certification? Choose the Scuba Diver Course for entry-level diving up to 12 meters with a pro.

Looking to dive independently? The Open Water Diver Course is the world's most popular scuba certification. In just 3 days, you'll learn essential skills and earn your license to dive with a buddy down to 18 meters – worldwide.

Learning to dive in the tropical waters of Koh Chang is ideal for couples, families, or solo travelers. With our small group sizes, modern equipment, and experienced multilingual instructors, your first dive experience will be safe, fun, and unforgettable.
