Prove Your Skills ‚Äì Become an Official SDI Instructor

The SDI Instructor Examination (IE) is the final step after your Instructor Development Course (IDC). This 2-day assessment will test your teaching abilities, diving knowledge, and leadership under pressure. Once passed, you'll be qualified to teach the full range of SDI entry-level courses.

‚Üí Learn more about becoming an SDI Instructor

What to Expect During the IE

  • Theory Exams on SDI teaching standards & dive theory
  • Classroom teaching presentations
  • Confined water demonstrations
  • Open water teaching presentations

After successful completion, the examiner submits your evaluation to SDI Headquarters for final approval and certification.

What You'll Be Allowed to Teach

Once certified as an SDI Open Water Scuba Instructor, you can independently teach and certify the following:

  • Discover SCUBA
  • Open Water Diver
  • Advanced Adventure Diver
  • Rescue Diver
  • First Aid / CPR (if certified as EFR Instructor)
  • Divemaster

To teach specialties (e.g. Deep, Wreck, Nitrox), you must hold the corresponding Specialty Instructor rating.

Specialty Instructor Example:

  • Nitrox Specialty = Nitrox Instructor certification
  • Wreck Specialty = Wreck Instructor certification
  • Deep Specialty = Deep Instructor certification

Upgrade your qualifications with an SDI Instructor Trainer at Chang Diving Center.

Course Requirements

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Completed SDI IDC training
  • Minimum 100 logged dives
  • Good health and strong water skills
  • Available for full 2-day exam program

Exam Schedule

  • Day 1: Orientation, pool presentations, written theory exams
  • Day 2: Open water teaching presentations + final evaluations

Please meet your examiner the day before at our dive center (4‚Äì5 PM) to finalize paperwork and prepare equipment.
