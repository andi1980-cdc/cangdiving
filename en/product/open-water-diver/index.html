What Is the Open Water Diver Course?

The Open Water Diver Course is your first full certification course. In just 3 days, you'll learn to dive safely, handle your equipment, and explore reefs up to 18 meters deep – anywhere in the world.

Our Instructors offer three eLearning options so you can choose what fits you best:

  • SDI eLearning: Browser-based, clear and mobile-friendly
  • PADI Basic: PDF manual + quizzes – simple & affordable
  • PADI eLearning: App-based, with videos & full interactivity

Who Can Join?

  • No prior experience required
  • Minimum age: 10 years
  • Ages 10–14 will receive a Junior Open Water Diver certification
  • Course content and exercises are the same – only depth limits differ
  • Reasonable fitness & swimming comfort
  • Duration: approx. 3 days

Was ist inklusive?

  • 3-day scuba certification course (SDI or PADI)
  • 4 open water dives + pool training
  • Professional instructor (1-on-1 to max. 1-on-4)
  • All scuba equipment included
  • Full digital materials or printed manual
  • Lunch, fruits, soft drinks, tea/coffee
  • Hotel pickup (available in selected areas of Koh Chang)
