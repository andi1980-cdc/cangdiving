SDI Instructor Development Course – Become a Diving Educator

Train to Teach – Inspire the Next Generation

Join our SDI Instructor Development Course (IDC) on Koh Chang and take the leap into professional scuba education. This 21-day program combines academic sessions, real teaching practice, and daily mentorship to prepare you for a global career in diving.

Whether in the classroom, pool, or ocean – you’ll learn how to confidently lead courses and adapt to your students' individual needs using the latest SDI materials.

→ Learn more about our Dive Professional Programs

What You’ll Learn

  • How to teach diving theory and practical skills
  • Conducting classroom, pool, and open water presentations
  • Evaluating and coaching student divers
  • Standards and safety procedures in diver education
  • Adapting to student needs and learning styles
  • Real-world teaching scenarios and team-based instruction

What’s Included

  • 21-day Instructor Development Program
  • Daily mentorship and personal feedback
  • Access to SDI teaching materials and training aids
  • Pool sessions, boat dives, workshops and classroom use
  • Skill demonstrations, student evaluations, presentation practice

❗ Note: SDI Instructor application & exam fees are not included.

Course Requirements

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Certified SDI Divemaster (or equivalent)
  • Minimum 100 logged dives
  • Good physical health and comfort in the water
  • Available for 21 full training days

Training Schedule

  • Theory sessions and course design
  • Confined water teaching in the pool
  • Open water training with student simulations
  • Final knowledge reviews and evaluations

Meet your instructor for orientation and equipment check before start. All training is done on-site and on our dive boats.
