Try Dive – Your First Step into the Underwater World

What Is a Try Dive?

Try Diving is a one-day, non-certification experience that lets you explore the underwater world — no license required. You'll complete two guided dives at calm, shallow reef sites around Koh Chang.

It's perfect for beginners who want to explore scuba diving before committing to a full course. Learn how it works in our full guide:

→ How a Try Dive works – Step by Step at Chang Diving

Who Can Join?

  • No prior experience required
  • Minimum age: 10 years
  • Reasonable physical fitness and swimming comfort
  • Onboard theory session — no classroom, no pool session

What's Included

  • 2 dives from our dive boat (max. 11 meters depth)
  • 1-on-1 to max. 1-on-4 instruction ratio
  • Full scuba gear (no hidden rental fees)
  • Hotel pickup (available in selected areas of Koh Chang)
  • Lunch, snacks, drinking water & fruits
  • Introductory dive theory & safety briefing

⚠️ Optional

  • Exclusive for our students & dive guests
  • Get a special discount on selected equipment
  • Mask, snorkel, fins & more available online
  • Book this equipment together with your course or trip and save instantly!
  • Offer valid only for combined online bookings on our website.
  • Dive accident insurance – recommended for all divers
  • ➡ Continue with our Open Water Diver Course (Option 3) and get certified!
