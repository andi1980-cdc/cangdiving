TDI Intro to Tech Course Introduction

Course Overview

This course introduces recreational divers to the world of technical diving. You’ll dive with sidemount or twinset configurations, improve buoyancy, propulsion, trim, and dive planning – all within no-decompression limits.

Key Topics: Gas planning, technical gear setup, situational awareness, physics & physiology of tech diving.

What You’ll Learn

  • Gear configuration with sidemount or twinset
  • Buoyancy, trim, propulsion techniques
  • Tech dive planning and situational awareness
  • Gas rules, SAC rate and cylinder labeling
  • Team diving protocols

Requirements

  • Agency: TDI Certification
  • Prerequisite: SDI Open Water Diver or equivalent
  • Logged Dives: Minimum 25
  • Minimum Age: 15 years
  • Duration: 2 days
  • Dives: 3 open water dives (max. depth: 23m)
  • Pool Session: Yes (Day 1)
  • Theory: Online
