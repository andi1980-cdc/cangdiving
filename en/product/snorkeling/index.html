Why Snorkel with Chang Diving Center?

Chang Diving Center is renowned for its professional diving excursions. Our spacious dive boat is fully equipped to take certified divers and snorkelers to the best underwater spots around Koh Chang. As a snorkeler, you will have the chance to explore the same captivating dive sites as the divers ‚Äì safely and comfortably.

Our snorkeling trips are perfect for families, beginners, and anyone who wants to enjoy the marine world in tropical waters. If this experience inspires you to explore further, you can join our Discover Scuba Diving program or proceed to the Open Water Diver course.

What to Expect?

Our day trips start in the morning with a pickup from your accommodation and take you to the best dive sites around Koh Chang. During the ride, you can relax and enjoy the sun on our spacious sundeck. Our dive boat provides ample space for comfort and relaxation as we travel to the most stunning locations in the area.

Snorkeling spots are home to vibrant marine life, including clownfish, turtles, moray eels, and stingrays. Learn more about the 10 most popular diving activities on Koh Chang.
