Solo Diver Course – Master Self-Reliant Diving

Why Solo Diving?

The SDI Solo Diver Course teaches you to dive safely and independently. It's the ideal choice for underwater photographers and experienced divers who want full control, better decision-making, and true underwater freedom.

→ Learn how specialty courses work at Chang Diving

What You'll Learn

  • Solo dive planning and gas management
  • Redundant gear and backup air source setup
  • Navigation and situational awareness underwater
  • Self-rescue and emergency protocols
  • Tips for underwater photographers and content creators

Your Options

  • Full Solo Diver Certification: 2 dives over 1 day + online theory
  • Optional: Camera & GoPro add-on (perfect for solo creatives)

Online theory included (SDI eLearning)

→ How specialty courses work at Chang Diving

Requirements

  • Agency: SDI
  • Minimum Age: 21 years
  • Certification: Advanced Open Water or equivalent
  • Logged Dives: 100 required
  • Duration: 1.5 days
  • Dives: 2 solo dives

What's Included?

  • 1-day Solo Diver Specialty course (SDI)
  • 2 Solo Diver dives from the boat
  • Professional instructor (1-on-1 to max. 1-on-4)
  • All scuba equipment included
  • Full digital materials or printed manual
  • Lunch, fruits, soft drinks, tea/coffee
  • Hotel pickup (available in selected areas of Koh Chang)
