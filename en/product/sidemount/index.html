Sidemount Diver Course – Dive Streamlined & Balanced

Why Sidemount Diving?

Sidemount diving lets you carry your tanks at your sides instead of your back – for better comfort, trim, and control. Originally used in cave and technical diving, it’s now a popular setup for recreational divers, especially those with back issues or technical aspirations.

→ Learn how specialty courses work at Chang Diving

What You’ll Learn

  • ⚙️ Streamlined equipment setup and gear trim
  • Buoyancy, balance, and gas management with two cylinders
  • Sidemount-specific dive planning and emergency procedures
  • How to switch regulators and manage redundancy
  • Ideal for wrecks, caves, and advanced exploration
