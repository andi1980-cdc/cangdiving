Navigation Specialty Diving Course Koh Chang | Improve Your Underwater Skills

Confident & independent underwater navigation

No more relying on your dive buddy – in this course, you will learn how to plan and complete your dives independently and safely.

The Navigation Specialty course on Koh Chang will teach you how to use a compass, natural navigation techniques, and patterns to always find your way underwater.

Perfect for those who want to take their diving skills to the next level – both on vacation and at home!

Why Koh Chang?

  • Tropical weather & warm waters
  • Calm, protected dive sites – ideal for practice
  • Great visibility & diverse marine life

Whether you’re here on holiday or for training – you’ll enjoy a professional & fun learning experience!

What You Will Learn

  • How to use an underwater compass
  • Navigating with natural references
  • Rectangular, triangular & U-shaped navigation patterns
  • Estimating distances underwater
  • Returning safely to the entry point

All taught hands-on by experienced instructors

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 day
  • 2 navigation dives from the boat
  • E-learning: Prepare from home at your pace
  • Theory sessions & briefings in a relaxed setting
  • Certification: Recognized worldwide

Requirements

  • ✅ Minimum age: 12 years
  • ✅ Minimum certification: Open Water Diver or equivalent
  • ✅ Last dive within 12 months – Check our Fresh-Up
