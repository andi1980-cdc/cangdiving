Search & Recovery Diver Course – Master Underwater Search

Why Learn Search & Recovery?

The Search & Recovery Diver Course teaches you how to locate and retrieve lost objects underwater – from dive gear and tools to treasures. Learn search patterns, knot tying, and how to use lift bags, all while building better navigation and planning skills.

→ How specialty courses work at Chang Diving

What You’ll Learn

  • Search patterns: expanding square, U-pattern & circular search
  • Knot tying for rigging lift bags and securing objects
  • Safe use of lift bags for recovery operations
  • Underwater navigation integration
  • Planning searches in low visibility or currents

Your Options

  • Full Search & Recovery Course: Includes full theory + 2 to 4 dives with search and lift practice
  • Adventure Dive (Search & Recovery): 1 day, 2 dives (basic object search training – counts toward AOWD)

Online theory access included (PADI PDF Manual or SDI eLearning)

Improve your precision with our Navigation Diver Course

Requirements

  • Agency: SDI (age 12+) or PADI (age 12+)
  • Prerequisite: PADI AOWD or SDI OWD
  • Dives: PADI – 4 dives | SDI – 2 dives
  • Duration: PADI – 2 days | SDI – 1 day

What's Included?

  • 2 to 4 Search & Recovery dives from the boat
  • Professional instructor (1-on-1 to max. 1-on-4)

  • Full digital materials or printed manual
