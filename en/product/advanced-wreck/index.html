Course Overview

This course is designed for divers who want to safely penetrate wrecks in an overhead environment. You will learn proper guideline use, propulsion techniques, gas management and redundancy setups to explore areas beyond the light zone ‚Äì or stay in the light zone with a flexible no-decompression training option.

Our training is conducted on the HTMS Chang, the largest wreck in Thailand, and includes additional training on T11 and PAK1 expeditions.
