Open Water to Divemaster Internship – Your Professional Dive Journey Starts Here

From Beginner to Pro – In One Complete Program

This two-month internship package is the ultimate way to go from zero experience to Divemaster. You'll complete all major certifications in a guided, structured environment with real exposure to how a dive center operates – in the water, in the office, and on the boat.

Important: This is a paid, full-service internship – not "free diving for work." You are investing in a professional transformation guided by our experienced instructors and team.

→ Learn more about Divemaster training at Chang Diving

What's Included

  • Open Water Diver Course (OWD)
  • Advanced Open Water Diver Course (AOWD)
  • Rescue Diver + First Aid Training
  • Divemaster Course (⚠️ crewpack and application fee not included)
  • 60 logged dives in warm tropical waters
  • Study materials for OWD, AOWD, Rescue, and First Aid
  • Daily briefings, logbook reviews, and dive site planning
  • Supervised support in dive shop, on board, and during logistics
  • Full mentorship by our multilingual instructor team

⚠️ Important: The required Divemaster crewpack and certification processing fee must be purchased separately.

Internship Duration & Schedule

The program is designed for approx. 60 days of immersion. Start dates are available between October and March to ensure enough diving and real-world training exposure during high season.

  • Weeks 1–2: OWD + AOWD + first dive site experience
  • Week 3: First Aid + Rescue training
  • Weeks 4–8: Divemaster development, skill workshops, daily diving

Days off and retakes are built into the schedule. You will dive often, study hard, and get involved – all while being part of the team!

Who Can Join?

  • No prior certification required
  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Good physical fitness & basic swimming ability
  • Commitment to teamwork, learning, and professionalism

Equipment Requirements

As a future dive professional, you'll need your own basic dive gear. We'll assist you in selecting the right equipment, and you can try various setups with us before committing. Special discounts available for Divemaster candidates.
