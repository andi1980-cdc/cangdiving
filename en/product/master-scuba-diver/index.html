Become a Master – Join the Elite

The Master Scuba Diver (MSD) rating is the most prestigious non-professional level in recreational diving. It's a recognition of your training, logged dives, and commitment to excellence underwater.

At Chang Diving, our certified instructors guide you step by step toward earning this recognition – whether you're following the PADI or SDI educational path.

*Please note: Master Scuba Diver is a certification level awarded by the respective training agency (PADI or SDI) upon meeting all program requirements. We offer full guidance and course support as certified instructors.

How to Qualify

PADI Master Scuba Diver

  • ✅ PADI Rescue Diver
  • ✅ 5 PADI Specialty Courses
  • ✅ 50 logged dives
  • ✅ Age: 18+ (or 12+ for Junior MSD)

SDI Master Diver

  • ✅ SDI Rescue Diver
  • ✅ 4 SDI Specialty Courses
  • ✅ 50 logged dives
  • ✅ Age: 18+

Important: You cannot mix certifications between agencies. PADI MSD = only PADI courses, SDI MSD = only SDI courses.
