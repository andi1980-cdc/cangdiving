Important to Know: What is an Adventure Dive?

Adventure Dives are not full Specialty Courses! Each Adventure Dive is the first dive of a corresponding Specialty course. If you wish to earn a full Specialty certification later, your Adventure Dive may be credited toward it.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does the Open Water + Advanced Open Water package take?
    Minimum 4 days ‚Äì including pool training and 9 dives from the boat.
  • Can I complete the theory before I arrive?
    Yes! We highly recommend completing your full Open Water theory beforehand. For Advanced Open Water, only the theory for your selected Adventure Dives needs to be completed.
  • Is Deep Diving and Navigation mandatory for AOWD?
    Yes, both Deep and Underwater Navigation dives are required for AOWD certification. You can choose your 3 remaining Adventure Dives freely.
  • What if I change my mind during the course?
    No problem ‚Äì if you complete OWD but wish to postpone AOWD, we can split your training and issue the certifications separately (additional costs may apply if courses are split significantly).
  • Do Adventure Dives count toward Specialty Courses?
    Yes! Each Adventure Dive is the first dive of a corresponding Specialty Course. It may be credited later if you continue with full Specialty certification (subject to instructor and agency approval).
  • What equipment is included?
    All necessary dive gear is included: BCD, regulator, wetsuit, mask, fins, tanks, weights. Nitrox tanks are included if you choose the Nitrox Adventure Dive ‚Äì no extra charge.
  • Can I choose Nitrox as an Adventure Dive?
    Absolutely! Nitrox Diving is one of the most popular Adventure Dives. It improves safety and bottom time for recreational diving ‚Äì and it‚Äôs included in your package if selected.
