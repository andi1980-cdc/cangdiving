At Chang Diving Center on Koh Chang, we provide flexible and professional Open Water Diver (OWD) training taught by certified SDI, TDI, and PADI instructors. You can choose between SDI or PADI certification – both are globally recognized and follow very similar standards in training and safety.

To make the most of your time on the island and secure your preferred course dates, we highly recommend booking your diving adventure in advance. It helps us prepare your theory access, schedule your pool session, and assign you the best possible instructor based on your experience level.

Choose Your Certification Agency – SDI or PADI?

Whether you choose PADI or SDI, your certification will be valid worldwide. The difference lies mainly in the theory platform and certification materials.

If you’d like a detailed comparison, check out our guide: SDI/TDI vs. PADI – What’s the Difference?.

We’re happy to help you decide which option fits your schedule and learning style best.

Theory Materials – SDI vs. PADI

Great question! Let’s break down the options for completing the theory portion of your first diving certification:

  1. SDI eLearning (Browser-Based):
    • Fully online, no app or downloads required – works in any web browser.
    • Straightforward, beginner-friendly design with clear, practical content.
    • SDI also offers free course videos on YouTube for review and preview.
    • Great for students who want easy access without extra costs for multimedia.
  2. PADI Basic (PDF + Online Exams):
    • You receive a downloadable PDF of the manual.
    • After reading, you take the quizzes and final exam online via the PADI platform.
    • Budget-friendly, but not interactive – no videos or guided media included.
    • Best for self-motivated learners who like reading at their own pace.
  3. PADI eLearning (Fully Interactive):
    • Includes videos, animations, and interactive quizzes integrated throughout the course.
    • Guided structure – ideal for those who enjoy multimedia and step-by-step learning.
    • Costs more, but provides a premium digital learning experience.

⏱️ Time Required:

All formats (PADI Basic, PADI eLearning, and SDI eLearning) take approximately 8 to 14 hours to complete, depending on your pace. Here’s how they compare:

  • SDI eLearning: Balanced, user-friendly, with free video access
  • PADI Basic: Requires more independent reading
  • PADI eLearning: Most interactive and streamlined
  • SDI: Browser-based online eLearning – simple, fast, mobile-friendly. ✅ Available in English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Russian, Polish, Czech, Japanese, Thai, and more (varies by course).
  • PADI: PDF manual + online exam via PADI portal. ✅ Available in over 20 languages including English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Dutch, Chinese (both), Japanese, Korean, Thai, Arabic, and more.

Note: Not all courses are available in every language – contact us if you're unsure about your preferred language.

What You’ll Learn – Full OWD Course: Skills in Pool & Open Water

All Open Water Diver students complete the same core skills, regardless of which option they booked (SDI or PADI). These skills are first practiced in the pool and then repeated and refined during open water dives.

Examples of skills include:

  • Buoyancy control and trim
  • Mask removal and clearing
  • Regulator recovery and out-of-air procedures
  • Controlled emergency ascent (CESA)
  • Underwater navigation with compass and natural reference

You can view the full skill overview in our downloadable OWD Skill Slate (PDF).

✔️ Included in the Open Water Course Price:

  • 1x Open Water Diver Manual (SDI or PADI – depending on option)
  • 1x Full day pool training (confined water)
  • 2x Boat days with a total of 4 open water dives
  • Full equipment rental, boat fees, instructor guidance

Option 2 – Scuba Diver course

The Scuba Diver course is the perfect option if you want to start your diving education but are not ready to commit to the full Open Water Diver course yet.

  • Duration: 2 days + theory (online)
  • Certification: Scuba Diver – valid to 12m with a professional

? What’s Included:

  • Online theory access (OWD Chapters 1–3) – SDI eLearning or PADI Basic Manual
  • Day 1: Swimming pool training (Confined Water Dives 1–3)
  • Day 2: 2 Open Water dives from the boat
  • Scuba Diver certification – worldwide recognition (PADI or SDI)

? Important Notes:

  • You are trained on the full OWD pool skill set – including referral form for upgrades
  • Upgrade to full Open Water Diver possible anytime – no need to repeat pool training if you continue within 12 months
  • Final exam is not required to receive Scuba Diver certification – you can delay theory completion

This course is ideal if you want flexibility and a real certification without the pressure of completing all 4 dives.

⚠️ Please note:
Scuba Divers must always dive with a dive professional and are limited to 12 meters.

Option 3 – Start with a Try Dive, Then Decide

Option 3 is ideal if you’re not yet sure whether you want to commit to a full Open Water Diver certification. It starts with a Try Dive experience – a safe, guided introduction to scuba diving with 2 dives from the boat.

During this day, you’ll already complete some basic scuba skills in real open water settings, such as shallow training areas or during surface intervals.

If you enjoy the Try Dive and want to continue:

  • Pool Day: You’ll complete the full Open Water skill set in a dedicated pool session (same as Option 1)
  • Final Day: You’ll complete 3 more open water dives to earn your Open Water Diver certification
  • Total: 5 dives in open water, including the initial Try Dive day

Important: If you decide to continue, you will need to complete all theory (PADI or SDI) within a short time frame after your Try Dive. This option gives you a great first taste of diving before committing to the full course – but also requires motivation to catch up on the academic part quickly.

Included in the base price of Option 3: 1x Try Dive day with 2 dives from the boat, full equipment, and instructor guidance.

Note: An additional upgrade fee is required if you decide to continue with the full Open Water Diver course (including pool session, 3 more dives, theory & certification).

⚠️ All prices are subject to change at any time without prior notice.
