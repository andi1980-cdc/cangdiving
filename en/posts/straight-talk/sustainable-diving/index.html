Pirm Udom Sub: Sustainable Diving on Koh Chang with Modern Technology

Diving on Koh Chang is an adventure that combines nature and excitement. With the Pirm Udom Sub, our specially converted dive boat, you can explore the stunning underwater world around Koh Chang in comfort and sustainability. Originally built as a marine patrol boat, the Pirm Udom Sub has been transformed into a modern platform for our diving expeditions.

From a Wooden Boat to the Pirm Udom Sub â€“ A Step Towards Sustainability

In 2020, we retired our large wooden boat, which accommodated up to 45 divers, to focus on a more efficient and sustainable model. We chose the Pirm Udom Sub, a marine patrol boat with a half-glider steel hull. After extensive modifications, it now meets all the requirements for safe, comfortable, and eco-friendly dive expeditions.

This change has helped us reduce our environmental impact while providing a more personalized and higher-quality diving experience for our guests.

Why the Pirm Udom Sub is Perfect for Our Diving Guests

  • Space for 36 tanks, 10 divers, and 3 crew members with full equipment.
  • The boat has a maximum capacity of 20 passengers + 2 crew members, but we limit occupancy to 10 divers and 3 crew for optimal comfort and safety.
  • A spacious dive deck at the stern for easy water access.
  • Stable and safe rides thanks to optimized weight distribution and trim.

Travel Data for Our Dive Trips:

Innovative Antifouling System for a Cleaner Environment

Our Pirm Udom Sub is equipped with advanced HD4 and HD1 Hull Shield Ultrasonic Antifouling Systems. This technology provides continuous protection against biofouling, keeping the hull clean without using harmful chemicals.

Benefits of the Hull Shield Technology:

  • Reduced Cleaning: Less effort to remove algae and marine life.
  • Lower Maintenance Costs: Longer lifespan of bottom paint and reduced repair expenses.
  • Effective Protection: Continuous impact on hulls, drives, rudders, and more.
  • Eco-Friendly: No chemical residues in the water.

Sustainability and Modern Technology Onboard

In addition to the innovative antifouling system, the Pirm Udom Sub features an integrated solar power system that reduces energy consumption and ensures reliable electricity onboard.

Our Solar Power System:

  • 1800-Watt Solar Panel System: Powers the main onboard systems.
  • 45-Watt Solar Panels: Separate chargers for consumer and starter batteries.
  • Inverter Charger (24V/200Ah): Enables the use of 220V power for lighting and electronic devices.
