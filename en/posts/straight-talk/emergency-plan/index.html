2. Contact Local Emergency Services

Koh Chang offers professional and fast assistance for diving accidents. Contact the following services:

3. Transport to the Nearest Decompression Chamber

If decompression treatment is needed, the victim should be transported to the nearest chamber as quickly as possible. The nearest chamber is located at:

  • Contact: +66 39 511 555

5. Prevention is Key

To minimize the risk of diving accidents, follow these key safety measures:

  • Adhere to your no-decompression limits and dive within your personal limits.
  • Plan your dives carefully and always perform a safety stop.
  • Listen to your body and never dive if you feel unwell.
  • Take an EFR (Emergency First Response) course to learn essential first aid skills.

Conclusion

Having a clear emergency plan and knowing how to act in case of a diving accident can save lives. Be prepared and share this emergency plan with your dive buddy. Safe dives start with good preparation and the right mindset!
