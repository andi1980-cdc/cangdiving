Straight Talk – Honest Insights into Diving on Koh Chang

Welcome to our "Straight Talk" section – where we speak plainly and honestly. No marketing fluff, no sugarcoating. This is where we share real insights from the diving world on Koh Chang, directly from our daily operations as a dive center. From sustainable technology and boat upgrades to training standards and safety protocols – we tell it like it is.

Here, you'll learn why we make certain decisions, what equipment we trust, and how we implement eco-friendly practices in our daily diving activities. We also tackle uncomfortable topics – like overtourism, fake reviews, and inadequate training practices – head-on.

Whether you're a curious beginner or an experienced diver, "Straight Talk" is your go-to space for clear, unfiltered perspectives. If you want to truly understand what happens behind the scenes – both underwater and onboard – this is where you'll find the answers.
