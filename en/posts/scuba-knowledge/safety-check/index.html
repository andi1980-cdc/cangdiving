Why is a Safety Check Important Before Diving on Koh Chang?

Koh Chang offers spectacular dive sites, vibrant reefs, and unforgettable underwater experiences. Before you dive into the magical underwater world, a thorough safety check is essential. At Chang Diving Center, we prioritize your safety and that of your dive buddy.

1. Equipment Check

A safety check ensures that your diving equipment is functioning properly before entering the water. This is especially important in tropical waters like those around Koh Chang, where conditions can vary:

  • Tank: Is it fully filled and securely fastened?
  • Regulator: Are both primary and alternate regulators functioning correctly?
  • BCD (Buoyancy Control Device): Does it inflate and deflate properly?
  • Weights: Are they securely attached and easily releasable in case of emergency?
  • Mask and fins: Are they comfortable and fitting properly?

2. Communication with Your Dive Buddy

The safety check is an excellent opportunity to strengthen teamwork with your dive buddy. Especially at amazing sites like the HTMS Chang wreck or the reefs around Koh Chang, clear communication is crucial. Review hand signals and discuss emergency scenarios together.

3. Accident Prevention

Many diving accidents are caused by avoidable problems, such as a loose weight belt or a faulty regulator. A thorough safety check helps identify these risks early so you can fully focus on the beauty of the underwater world.

4. Mental Preparation

A safety check not only allows you to ensure your equipment is ready but also gives you the time to mentally prepare for your dive. For more advanced dives, such as a Nitrox dive, a clear head is essential.

5. Avoiding Panic

In stressful situations, properly functioning equipment can make all the difference. Knowing your equipment is safe allows you to enter the water confidently and enjoy your dive around Koh Chang stress-free.
