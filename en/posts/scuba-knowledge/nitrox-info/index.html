Understanding NITROX for Scuba Diving

NITROX, also known as Enriched Air, is a breathing gas blend with a higher oxygen percentage than standard air, which contains 21% oxygen and 79% nitrogen. For recreational scuba diving, NITROX typically starts at 22% oxygen, with blends up to 40% available for certified divers.
