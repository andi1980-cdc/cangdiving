1. Characteristics of Blacktip Reef Sharks

  • Max Length: 1.5 to 2 meters
  • Body Shape: Slim, with a dark back and white underside
  • Distinctive Markings: Black tips on the first dorsal fin and fin edges
  • Habitat: Shallow coral reefs, mangroves, and coastal waters

2. Behavior & Lifestyle

  • Very shy: Quickly disappears when they sense people.
  • Hunts near coastlines: Mainly feeds on fish, small sharks, and squid.
  • Active both day and night: Capable of hunting anytime.
  • No aggression towards divers: Completely harmless to humans.

4. Why Are Blacktip Reef Sharks Rarely Seen?

  • Often remain in shallow, protected areas.
  • Overfishing has significantly reduced their population.
