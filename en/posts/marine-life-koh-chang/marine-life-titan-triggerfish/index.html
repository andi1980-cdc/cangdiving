Titan Triggerfish in Koh Chang â€“ Behavior, Dangers & Best Dive Sites

The Titan Triggerfish (Balistoides viridescens) is one of the most fascinating yet misunderstood fish in tropical waters. While divers love its vibrant colors, it is also known for its strong territorial behavior. In Koh Chang, you can frequently encounter them at various dive sites.

1. Characteristics & Biology

  • Scientific Name: Balistoides viridescens
  • Size: Up to 75 cm
  • Weight: Up to 10 kg
  • Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Special Features: Powerful jaws with strong teeth for breaking corals and shells

2. Behavior & Lifestyle

The Titan Triggerfish is highly territorial, especially during nesting season. Divers should be aware of its conical defense zone: The fish defends its territory in an upside-down cone shape.

What to do if it gets aggressive?

  • Swim calmly to the side, not upwards!
  • Avoid sudden movements
  • Respect its space

3. Diet & Feeding Habits

The Titan Triggerfish has a diverse diet, including:

  • Coral
  • Small fish
  • Shellfish
  • Mollusks & sea urchins

4. Best Dive Sites to See the Titan Triggerfish in Koh Chang

The Titan Triggerfish is found almost everywhere around Koh Chang, but the most popular reef for Titan Triggerfish is Hin Raab South.
