Health & Safety – Frequently Asked Questions

🏥 Do I need a medical certificate to dive?
In most cases, you just need to fill out a medical questionnaire. A doctor's certificate is only needed for specific conditions. 📄 ➜ View medical questionnaire form
👓 Can I dive with glasses or contact lenses?
Yes! Contact lenses are usually fine. We also offer masks with prescription lenses. 💬 ➜ Ask about prescription masks
🛡️ Is diving insurance required?
Diving insurance is highly recommended. You can get day or annual coverage at low cost. ℹ️ ➜ Learn about diving insurance options
🚨 What is the emergency protocol at Chang Diving?
In the rare event of a diving accident, we follow a clear and structured emergency plan. Our staff is trained, we carry first aid and oxygen onboard, and we have direct access to the decompression chamber in Trat. 🚑 ➜ View our emergency response plan
