👥 Do you dive in groups or individually?

We keep it personal: small groups with max. 5 divers per guide – or private dives on request. Experienced buddy teams are welcome to explore the dive site independently after the dive briefing. Certified solo divers with proper equipment are also allowed to dive solo. ➜ Learn more about solo diving requirements | ➜ Contact us for private dive arrangements

🎖️ I'm a CMAS 1★ diver – can I dive with you?

Yes, absolutely! We recognize all internationally accepted certifications, including CMAS, SSI, RAID, PADI, NAUI, and others. As a CMAS 1★ diver, you're welcome to join our fun dives or training programs. If it's been a while since your last dive, we may recommend a refresher.

Important: CMAS certifications cannot be verified online, so please don't forget to bring your certification card and logbook with you.

🔄 Do I need a refresher to dive with you?

If you haven't been diving in the last 12 months, we strongly recommend a ➜ refresher course. This ensures your safety, boosts your confidence, and allows us to give you the best diving experience. It includes theory review, skills session, and two easy dives – no pressure, just comfort 😊

Can I do a refresher and dive the HTMS Chang on the same day?

Unfortunately, no. Since the HTMS Chang is a deep dive, it must be done as the first dive of the day due to decompression safety. A refresher (scuba review) should never be combined with a deep dive. We recommend diving for two days: Day 1: Refresher with two dives at easier dive sites. Day 2: Dive the HTMS Chang.
