How long does a dive last?

The duration of a dive depends on several factors ‚Äì especially depth, your air consumption, and local conditions.

At Chang Diving, we use standard S80 aluminum tanks (11.1 liters / 80 cuft at 200 bar), which corresponds to about 2220 liters of breathing gas.

On average, around 1665 liters can be actively used while diving, with the rest kept as a safety reserve.

Under normal conditions, the typical dive time is 50‚Äì60 minutes. Depending on breathing rate, depth, and current, actual dive time can be shorter or longer.

What are the visibility conditions like when diving in Koh Chang?

Visibility around Koh Chang averages between 8 and 20 meters ‚Äì depending on the season, current, and dive site.
The best conditions are usually between October and May when the sea is calm and there's little plankton.

During the rainy season (June to September), visibility may be reduced, but there are fewer boats ‚Äì ideal for wreck fans and macro photography.
