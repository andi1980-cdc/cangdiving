General Questions About Diving in Koh Chang

🏊‍♂️ Why is Koh Chang a great diving destination in Thailand?

Warm water, healthy reefs and a relaxed atmosphere make Koh Chang ideal for divers of all levels.

📅 When is the best time to go diving in Koh Chang?

The main season runs from October to May with calm seas and good visibility.

Related FAQs: ➜ Weather Info | ➜ Safety Guidelines

🌡️ What are the water temperatures and visibility like?

Expect water temperatures between 28°C and 30°C, with visibility ranging from 10 to 20 meters.

🐠 What marine life can I see while diving in Koh Chang?

You might spot 🐢 turtles, moray eels, pufferfish, seahorses, nudibranchs, stingrays and even the occasional 🐋 whale shark. Koh Chang's marine biodiversity is stunning!
