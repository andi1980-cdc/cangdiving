How long does the Open Water Course take?

Usually 3 to 4 days, depending on your pace and weather conditions. We are flexible with scheduling to ensure you get the most out of your training!

How does the theory part of my course work?

The theory is 100% online – no classroom needed. You can learn from home or your beach bungalow. It is available in all major languages supported by your chosen training agency (SDI, PADI, TDI, etc.).
