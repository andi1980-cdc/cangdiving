Welcome to Chang Diving Center, your trusted diving partner in Koh Chang since 2005.

Located at the beautiful Klong Prao Beach, we are proud to offer world-class scuba diving experiences in one of Thailand's most stunning underwater environments. Whether you're a complete beginner, an experienced diver, or seeking technical diving challenges, we have something special for everyone.

Our team of professional instructors is passionate about diving and committed to your safety, comfort, and growth as a diver. We offer a wide range of courses, from beginner-level certifications to advanced technical training, all designed to meet international standards. Our fun dives are perfect for those who want to explore the vibrant marine life and captivating dive sites that Koh Chang is famous for.

At Chang Diving Center, we believe that diving is more than just earning a certification card ‚Äì it's about building skills, confidence, and unforgettable memories. Our mission is to help you become a knowledgeable and confident diver, ready to explore the underwater world with ease. Join us at Chang Diving Center and discover the wonders of Koh Chang's underwater paradise. Let us guide you on a journey that will inspire your love for the ocean and create memories that last a lifetime!

Our Journey

  • 2005: Founded as (Scandinavian) Chang Dive Center on Koh Chang (View).
  • 2009: Achieved the status of a 5-star dive center, offering certifications from leading international diving organizations.
  • 2014: Became one of the first dive centers in Koh Chang to implement environmentally friendly diving practices.
  • 2016: Expanded our offerings to include TDI/SDI Technical Diving courses.
  • 2020: Became an independent dive center, offering courses from SDI, TDI, and taught by certified PADI instructors (What Does "Independence" Mean for You as Our Guests?).
  • 2025: Celebrate the 20th Anniversary of Chang Diving

Why Dive with Chang Diving Center?

  • Experienced Instructors: Certified PADI, SDI, and TDI trainers who prioritize safety and skill development.
  • Custom-Built Dive Boat: A spacious dive boat designed to host 12 divers with up to 3 dives per day.
  • Comprehensive Courses: Choose from fun dives, open water certification, and advanced technical diving courses.
