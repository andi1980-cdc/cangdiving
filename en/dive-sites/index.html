Dive Sites around Koh Chang

Wrecks, reefs & pinnacles – discover Thailand's top dive spots with us.

Explore Koh Chang's Best Dive Sites

Discover the top dive sites in Koh Chang's national marine park – from shallow reefs to thrilling wrecks and deep pinnacles. Whether you're a beginner or a tech diver, our guided trips offer the best underwater experiences in Thailand. Small groups, professional crew, and unforgettable memories await beneath the surface.

🗺️ Dive Site Map Koh Chang

Explore Koh Chang's top dive sites with our dive site map. From wreck dives at the HTMS Chang to coral reefs like Hin Luk Bat.

🏔️ Hin Raab North

Dive at Hin Raab North, Koh Chang's hidden treasure. Spot reef sharks, explore coral canyons, and experience vibrant marine life. Book fun dives and courses!

🏝️ Hin Sam Sao

Discover Hin Sam Sao, Koh Chang's hidden gem. Explore coral gardens, shallow reefs, and marine life like stingrays and moray eels. Book fun dives today!

🐠 Hin Luk Bat

Dive at Hin Luk Bat, Koh Chang's top-rated dive site. Swim through coral walls, spot clownfish, and explore vibrant anemone fields. Book your fun dive now!

🚢 HTMS Chang Wreck

Dive into the HTMS Chang, Thailand's largest shipwreck. Explore cargo holds, marine life, and adventure. Wreck diving courses available at Koh Chang.

⚓ Phutthayotfa Chulalok-Wreck

Dive into the Phutthayotfa Chulalok frigate wreck near Koh Chang. At 40 meters depth, this 134-meter-long warship offers an unforgettable experience for advanced and technical divers

🫐 Blueberry Hill

Dive into Blueberry Hill, Koh Chang's hidden gem. Explore coral canyons, tropical fish, and stunning pinnacles. Perfect for fun dives and scuba courses.

🛥️ T11 Wreck

Discover the T11 wreck, a small patrol boat resting at 18m depth. Perfect for wreck diving beginners and marine life encounters.

🤫 Secret Reef

Dive at Secret Reef, Koh Chang's hidden treasure. Explore stunning coral canyons, spot moray eels, stingrays, and discover the mystery of the ancient teapot.

🏔️ Hin Raab South

Dive at Hin Raab South, Koh Chang's iconic reef dive. Spot moray eels, blue-spotted rays, triggerfish, and turtles while exploring stunning coral gardens.

🏊 Hin Rua Tek

Dive at Hin Rua Tek, Koh Chang's famous coral wall. Explore hidden crevices, spot reef sharks, stingrays, barracudas, and stunning marine life.

🏔️ Koh Rang Pinnacle

Explore Koh Rang Pinnacle, a deep dive site at Koh Chang's Marine Park. Dive 30m deep, spot eagle rays, giant trevallies, and vibrant coral gardens.

🪨 Hin Pray Nam

Dive Hin Pray Nam at Koh Chang. Discover coral walls, reef sharks & stingrays. A must-dive site for lovers of vibrant Thai marine life.

🚢 Koho Maru 5

Dive into the Koho Maru 5 Pak1, a rare deep wreck near Koh Chang. Discover its unique history and challenge your technical diving skills.
